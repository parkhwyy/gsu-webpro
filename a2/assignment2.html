  • Overview

    • Preparation time: 30 Minutes
    • Cooking time: 30 Minutes
    • Total: 1 hour
    • Servings: 2-3


  • Ingredients

    • 1.5 lb sliced pork shoulder
    • 1 onion
    • 1 whole garlic
    • 1 green onion
    • 5T red pepper powder
    • 5T olive oil
    • 5T soy sauce
    • 3T cooking wine
    • 2T sugar
    • 1.5T sesame oil
    • 1T plum extract
    • 1T minced garlic
    • 0.5T black pepper


  • Directions

    1. Preparing marinade
      Mix soy sauce, cooking wine, sugar, sesame oil, plum extract, minced garlic, and black pepper.
    2. Marinating the meat
      Pour the marinade into the sliced pork and mix them thoroughly.
      Wrap the bowl and keep it in the fridge for about 30 minutes.
    3. Preparing veggies
      Wash and slice the onion, garlics, and green onions.
    4. Stir-frying
      Heat the pan and add the oil, garlics, and green onion (1/2).
      Once they start to turn brown, put the marinated pork into the pan.
      After about 10 minutes, add the onion, remaining green onions, and red pepper powder.
      Stir-fry them for another 10 minutes.
      Put on a plate and finish with a sprinkle of sesame seeds.
